About Gisenyi

Gisenyi is a beautiful resort town located on the northern shore of Lake Kivu, in western Rwanda. Known for its stunning lake views, vibrant culture, and pleasant climate, it's a popular destination for both local and international tourists.

The town offers a variety of attractions including beautiful beaches, hot springs, and the nearby Volcanoes National Park, home to the famous mountain gorillas.
